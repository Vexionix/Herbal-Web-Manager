How to use the website

Each user must create an account that they will use to login. After logging in, a user can use our multi-criteria search in order to find out more about the beauty of nature in terms of plants that cover a wide domain area. You can sign out after logging in, but you will lose access to most features. Being logged-in also gives you the right to post both images and tracking progress such as collected plants.

If you encounter a problem on our website, we would appreciate it if you would fill out the form found in the Contact tab. The site is still in a very incipient state and as such there might be a lot of bugs/unimplemented features for now.
