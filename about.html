About HeMa

Herbal Web Manager (HeMa) is a web application that offers support for managing collections of graphic representations of plants alongside of their attributes. Featuring a multi-criteria search system, the site will generate, recommend and share thematic albums that contain medicinal plants, mountain flowers etc. Statistics can be exported as CSV and PDF. The site also displays a leaderboard consisting of the most collected plants.
